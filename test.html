SUGGESTED HOME FOR YOU

About Bedrooms

  1. Bedrooms should contain 2 windows Side-by-side or opposite to each other for proper ventilation and Light in Day

  2. Bedrooms should contain only 2 LED lights of 15W for good lightning in Night

  3. Use bright colour on the walls of the room so the light reflects from the wall and get a better light in room in day and night both


About Balcony

  1. One Balcony should be East facing and one should be west facing so and attach to the bedrooms, so you can get fresh air and U can take sunbath in winter and in the Summer due to proper vantilation you will not feel hot in your rooms (Remember balcony should be in the opposite of the Windows)


About dining-room

  1. You should use glass doors and windows so the light can enter in the room and no need to turn on the light in day specially in sunny day.

  2. Use 5 lights, 4 on the corners and 1 in the center of 15W LED ligths.

  3. Don't use dark paint in the dining room.
